About Caenorhabditis virilis

Caenorhabditis afra, formally sp. 7, is a male-female species in the Elegans supergroup. It was originally isolated in 2007 by Matthias Herrmann in Begoro, Ghana. The genome of an inbred strain, JU1286, was sequenced in 2014 and assembled by Matt Newton (Blaxter Lab).
