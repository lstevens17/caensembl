About Caenorhabditis virilis

Caenorhabditis virils, formally sp. 13, is a male-female species in the Drosophilae supergroup. It was originally isolated in 2008 from an apple orchard in France by Marie-Anne Felix. The genome of an inbred strain, JU1956, was sequenced in 2014 and assembled by Georgios Koutsovoulos (Blaxter Lab).
