About Caenorhabditis sp. 1

Caenorhabditis sp. 1 is a male-female species which is an outgroup to all known Caenorhabditis species. It was originally discovered living on the fungus Ganoderma applanatum, possibly having formed a phoretic relationship with Cis beetles. The genome of an inbred strain, JU1667, was sequenced in 2014 and assembled by Lewis Stevens (Blaxter Lab).
